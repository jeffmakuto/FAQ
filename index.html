Executive Summary

In the pursuit of fostering a culture of ethics and integrity within Kenya Airways, the Ethics and Integrity FAQ Bot is an innovative solution designed to guide employees through ethical decision-making.

Background

Problem Statement: Kenya Airways faces the challenge of instilling a culture of ethics and integrity among its employees due to the absence of a centralized resource for addressing ethical concerns.

Rationale: Addressing this challenge is crucial for maintaining the airline's reputation, compliance, employee satisfaction, and attracting stakeholders who prioritize ethical business conduct.

Objectives

Primary Objective: To serve as a comprehensive and interactive tool offering guidance on ethical issues, dilemmas, and integrity-related concerns.

Secondary Objectives:

  • Enhance employee understanding of Kenya Airways' Integrity code, mission, vision, and values.
  • Foster a proactive approach to ethical decision-making.
  • Establish a feedback loop for continuous improvement of the FAQ Bot's content and functionality.
