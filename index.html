Executive Summary

In the pursuit of fostering a culture of ethics and integrity within Kenya Airways, the Ethics and Integrity FAQ Bot is an innovative solution designed to guide employees through ethical decision-making.

Background

Problem Statement: Kenya Airways faces the challenge of instilling a culture of ethics and integrity among its employees due to the absence of a centralized resource for addressing ethical concerns.

Rationale: Addressing this challenge is crucial for maintaining the airline's reputation, compliance, employee satisfaction, and attracting stakeholders who prioritize ethical business conduct.

Objectives

Primary Objective: To serve as a comprehensive and interactive tool offering guidance on ethical issues, dilemmas, and integrity-related concerns.

Secondary Objectives:

  • Enhance employee understanding of Kenya Airways' Integrity code, mission, vision, and values.
  • Foster a proactive approach to ethical decision-making.
  • Establish a feedback loop for continuous improvement of the FAQ Bot's content and functionality.

Functionality - Version 1

On KQ World, there will be a section “Ethics and Integrity”, under the legal department. When anyone clicks that part, there’s a redirect to a new page just how the Ipride Internal button works. Once in the new page, no login or registration is required to ensure anonymity and privacy is achieved. To this extent, an obfuscation mechanism will be needed to ensure details associating a user to the KQ World is hidden. This allows employees to interact with the bot freely.

A page similar to the above will be displayed. Any mouse hovering over the Mission, Vision and Values will display the meaning of each. The page will also display upcoming Ethics and Integrity events and campaigns, and whistle–blowing contacts to use.

Whenever the page loads, the bot will be activated and display an initial greeting before allowing interactions with the employees accessing it. There will also be a maximize button that centers the bot and makes its chat box bigger.
