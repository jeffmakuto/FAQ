Executive Summary

In the pursuit of fostering a culture of ethics and integrity within Kenya Airways, the Ethics and Integrity FAQ Bot is an innovative solution designed to guide employees through ethical decision-making.

Key Features

Initial Bot image

Whenever the page loads, the bot will be activated and display an initial greeting of 3 seconds before allowing interactions with the employees accessing it. There will also be a maximize button that centers the bot and makes its chat box bigger.

Mission-Vission-Values Image

The mission and vision boxes have the name at the front with their definitions at the back. The boxes rotate 360 degrees after 3 seconds, displaying each side. There's also the SCIA Values on the left side. Focus is put on one value at a time and as such, others remain faint while one is bold to show which is the focus value. So there's that iteration.

Bot Functionality

the Bot provides an input section where one can fill their query. If the bot doesn't understand the query it replies with a default that it doesn't understand. If it understands then it gives the correct response. To send the message you click send. The sent message displays on the message container as "You:..." and the bot's reply as "Bota:...". To clear messages on the message container you click the clear button.

About

This project was inspired by a personal journey toward fostering a culture of ethics and integrity within the Kenya Aiways organization. Currently, there's need to educate staff on the new mission, vision, values and integrity code launched in November 2023. There are also concerns on sexual harrassment and need for guaidance on ways to report such matters discretely. It is not just a project for Holberton School; it is a passion-driven initiative to contribute to a better workplace where integrity and ethics are paramount.

The project timeline reflects months of dedicated effort, balancing coursework and real-world application. As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, this endeavor represents not only technical skills but a commitment to values that go beyond code.
